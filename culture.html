Festival

India celebrates a variety of festivals that refect its rich cultural hertigate and religious diversity:
  • Diwali: Festival of light, symbolizing the victory of light over darkness.
  • Holi: Festival of colour, celebrating love and arrival of spring.
  • Eid: Festival of harmony, marked by prayers, feasts and charity.
  • Christmas: Festival of joy, ccelebrating the birth of Jesus Christ.
